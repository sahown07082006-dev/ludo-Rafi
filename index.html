How to Play

Premium Offline Ludo rules & new point features

✦ Starting: Players need to roll a 6 to release a token from the yard into the common starting grid cell.

✦ Extra Rolls: Getting a 6, capturing an opponent's token, or reaching the center goal grants an extra turn.

✦ Safe Zones: Cells containing gold stars and player starting cells are safe. Token captures cannot occur here.

✦ 🎯 Bonus Movement Points (New Feature):
• Earning: Capturing an opponent's token earns you +1 Point (Max 4 Points).
• Losing: If an opponent captures your token, you lose 1 Point per token captured.
• Usage: Spend your points (1 Point = 1 Step) to advance an active token forward during your turn.
• Restrictions: Bonus Points CANNOT be used to capture opponent tokens, and CANNOT be used to release tokens from the yard.

✦ ⚡ Cosmic Powers (Capture Bonus): Every 4 pawn captures grants you 1 Power Charge. You can stack charges and use them anytime to:
• Force Six: Get a 6 on your next roll.
• Leap (+6): Instantly advance an active pawn 6 spaces forward.
• Aegis Safe: Teleport an active pawn directly to the next upcoming Safe Zone.

✦ 👥 2v2 Team Play: Red & Yellow (Team Alpha) vs. Green & Blue (Team Beta). Partners bypass each other's home spaces without capturing, and can overlap freely. When one partner finishes, they can help roll and move their teammate's pawns!
